Best Country to Create a Startup

Why Create a Startup Outside of Your Home Country

Starting a startup in a foreign country can offer numerous advantages. These may include access to larger or more receptive markets, favorable regulatory environments, better funding opportunities, exposure to new ideas and technologies, and potential tax benefits. Additionally, some countries offer special visas or incentives to attract foreign entrepreneurs, fostering an ecosystem that supports innovation and growth.

Success Stories of Startups Created In a Foreign Country

  • Wise: Taavet Hinrikus from Estonia established Wise in London. The company provides low-cost international money transfers.
    Source: Wise origin story
  • Nubank: David Vélez from Colombia founded Nubank in São Paulo. Nubank offers digital banking services with no-fee credit cards.
    Source: Techcrunch Nubank origin story
  • Slack: Stewart Butterfield from Canada created Slack in San Francisco. Slack is a platform for team communication and collaboration.
    Source: Techcrunch Slack origin stiry

    Economic Indicators of Countries

    Looking into the countries profiles we can model our business in 3 ways:

  • Luxury: Marketing ourselves to the citizens of country with high income. Although their population is smaller their purchasing power justifies it.
  • Mass Market: Countries with high PPP and high population have middle PPP per capita levels. We should have affordable pricing and scalable business.
  • High Risk High Reward: Offering specialized products to the least developed countries with low PPP and PPP per capita comes with a high risk. Business owner need to navigate through their undeveloped infraststructures and weak rule of law.

    • Here are some important insights among the major economies:

  • PPP growth: The top 5 countries with highest PPP growth from 2013 to 2023 are India, Bangladesh, Indonesia, China, and Nigeria.
  • PPP per capita growth: The top 5 countries with highest PPP growth from 2013 to 2023 are India, Bangladesh, Vietnam, Philipines, and Turkey.
  • India's Potential: India appears again in this section showing potential. With its high economic growth, huge market, and strong startup ecosystems.
